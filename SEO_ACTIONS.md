# HPL KSA SEO Operations Checklist

## Google Indexing

- Add the real Google Search Console token to `NEXT_PUBLIC_GOOGLE_SITE_VERIFICATION`.
- Deploy the site, then verify `https://hplksa.com` in Google Search Console.
- Submit `https://hplksa.com/sitemap.xml`.
- Use URL Inspection for the Arabic homepage, the four main service pages, and the Riyadh/Jeddah/Dammam pages.

## Priority Keywords To Track

- `قواطع حمامات HPL`
- `قواطع حمامات HPL السعودية`
- `قواطع حمامات HPL الرياض`
- `قواطع حمامات HPL جدة`
- `قواطع حمامات HPL الدمام`
- `لوكرات HPL`
- `كبائن استحمام HPL`
- `ألواح HPL السعودية`
- `قواطع فينوليك السعودية`

## Backlink And Citation Targets

- Create or complete Google Business Profile, Bing Places, and Apple Business Connect.
- Add consistent NAP citations using:
  - Company name: `NEW BASIC Company`
  - Phone: `+966 551 130 855`
  - Email: `newbasic.ac@gmail.com`
  - Website: `https://hplksa.com`
- Request links from contractors, suppliers, interior fit-out partners, and completed-project stakeholders.
- Submit the business to Saudi construction, fit-out, building materials, and supplier directories.
- Publish short project case studies that partners can reference and link to.

## Content Expansion Queue

- Add project case studies with city, sector, scope, material, installation timeline, and result.
- Add real client logos only when permission exists.
- Add city pages after proof is available for: مكة، المدينة، الخبر، الجبيل، تبوك، أبها.
- Add comparison content for HPL vs MDF, PVC, aluminum, and compact laminate alternatives.

## Monthly Review

- Check GSC indexing status for all sitemap URLs.
- Review queries with impressions but low CTR and rewrite titles/meta descriptions.
- Review pages ranking positions 8-20 and add internal links from relevant pages.
- Record Core Web Vitals field data and investigate any LCP, CLS, or INP warnings.
